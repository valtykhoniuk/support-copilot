# FoxSchool Release Notes — v2.0

**Release date:** December 15, 2025

---

## New Features

### Advanced plan launch (C1–C2)
New **Advanced** subscription tier at $40/month unlocks C1 and C2 content—44 video lessons with advanced grammar, idioms, and professional communication modules.

### Placement test
Free 15-minute placement test recommends your starting module across all levels. Available at app.foxschool.app/placement before or after signup.

### Three-device limit
Accounts now support up to 3 simultaneous browser sessions. Fourth login automatically ends the oldest session.

## Improvements

### Video CDN migration
All lesson videos migrated to AWS CloudFront (eu-west-1). Average buffering events reduced by 60% for EU users.

### Refund policy clarity
Help Center refund article updated with explicit 7-day full-refund window and mentor session cancellation rules (24-hour policy).

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed login redirect loop on Edge 109
- Fixed Intermediate plan not showing B2 Module 1 after B1 completion
- Fixed mentor session calendar daylight-saving bug for US timezones

## Known Issues

- Certificate PDF generation may take up to 2 minutes during peak hours. Resolved in v2.1.
