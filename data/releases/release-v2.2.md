# FoxSchool Release Notes — v2.2

**Release date:** June 1, 2026

---

## New Features

### Level final exams
Each CEFR level (A1–C2) now includes a final exam with 50 questions. Pass at 80% to unlock optional completion certificate purchase ($15).

### Dashboard streak grace day
One grace day per week—missing a single day no longer breaks your streak immediately. Grace days reset every Monday 00:00 UTC.

### French pronunciation guide
New phonetic overlay for all French A1 and A2 video lessons. Toggle in the video player under **CC → Pronunciation**.

## Improvements

### Mentor booking
Average wait time for mentor slots reduced from 4 days to **2 business days**. Added email reminder 1 hour before session.

### Billing transparency
Invoice history now shows proration line items separately when upgrading mid-cycle.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed speaking exercise WebRTC failure on Safari 17.0–17.3
- Fixed progress ring showing 100% before final exam completion
- Fixed duplicate email on mentor session cancellation refund
- Fixed German B1 Module 5 video subtitle sync offset

## Known Issues

- Offline mode is not available—lessons require an active internet connection. Under investigation for Q4 2026.
