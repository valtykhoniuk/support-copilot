# Login Problems

If you cannot log in to FoxSchool, try these steps before contacting support.

## Forgot password

1. Go to [app.foxschool.app/forgot-password](https://app.foxschool.app/forgot-password)
2. Enter your registered email
3. Click the reset link within **1 hour** (link expires after 60 minutes)
4. Create a new password (8+ characters, 1 number minimum)

Reset emails arrive within 3 minutes. Check spam folder for `noreply@foxschool.app`.

## Email not recognized

- Ensure you are using the exact email from signup (no typos, correct domain)
- One email = one account; you cannot merge accounts
- If you signed up with Google OAuth, use **Continue with Google** instead of email/password

## Account locked

After **5 failed login attempts** in 15 minutes, your account locks for 30 minutes. You receive an email with unlock instructions.

To unlock immediately: click the link in the lockout email or contact support@foxschool.app.

## Email verification required

New accounts must verify email within 48 hours. Without verification:

- Dashboard loads but lessons do not play
- Banner shows: "Verify your email to start learning"

Click **Resend verification** on the dashboard or login page.

## Browser issues

| Symptom | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Blank page after login | Clear cache and cookies for foxschool.app |
| Redirect loop | Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers) |
| "Session expired" immediately | Enable cookies; check system clock is correct |

## Two-factor authentication

FoxSchool does not offer 2FA as of March 2026.

## Still stuck?

Email support@foxschool.app with:

- Your registered email
- Screenshot of the error message
- Browser name and version

Support responds Monday–Friday, 9:00–18:00 UTC.
