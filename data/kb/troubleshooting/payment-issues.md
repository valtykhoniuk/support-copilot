# Payment and Billing Issues

Common billing problems and how to resolve them on FoxSchool.

## Declined card

If your payment fails at signup or renewal:

1. Verify card number, expiry, and CVV
2. Ensure sufficient funds (FoxSchool charges in USD)
3. Contact your bank—international or recurring charges may be blocked
4. Retry at **Settings → Billing → Update payment method**

Failed renewals retry automatically on **day 3** and **day 7**. Access suspends on **day 8** until payment succeeds.

## Double charge

If you see two identical charges on the same day:

1. Check if one is a **pending authorization** (disappears in 3–5 business days)
2. If both are posted charges, email support@foxschool.app with bank statement screenshot
3. Duplicate charges are refunded within **5 business days**

FoxSchool never charges twice for the same billing period unless you manually upgraded (proration creates a separate line item).

## Wrong plan charged

| Situation | Resolution |
|-----------|------------|
| Charged $30 instead of $20 | You selected Intermediate; downgrade at next renewal |
| Upgrade proration looks wrong | Proration = (new price − old price) × days remaining / 30 |
| Mentor session charged twice | Email support with booking confirmation IDs |

## Updating payment method

1. **Settings → Billing → Payment method**
2. Add new card or PayPal
3. Set as default
4. Old method is removed after next successful charge

## Invoices and receipts

- Receipts emailed after every charge from `billing@foxschool.app`
- Download past invoices: **Settings → Billing → Invoice history** (last 24 months)

## PayPal-specific issues

- PayPal subscriptions must be cancelled in both FoxSchool **and** PayPal if you want to stop renewals
- PayPal disputes: contact support@foxschool.app before opening a PayPal claim

## Tax and VAT

Prices shown are exclusive of VAT. EU customers may see VAT added at checkout (rate depends on country).
