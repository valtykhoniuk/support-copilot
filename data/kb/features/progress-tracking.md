# Progress Tracking and Dashboard

Your FoxSchool dashboard shows real-time learning progress, quiz scores, and study streaks.

## Dashboard sections

| Section | What it shows |
|---------|---------------|
| **Continue learning** | Next unlocked lesson |
| **Progress ring** | % of current level completed |
| **Quiz scores** | Average and per-module breakdown |
| **Streak** | Consecutive days with at least 1 lesson completed |
| **Time studied** | Total minutes this week and this month |

## How progress is calculated

- **Lesson complete:** all exercises finished + quiz passed at 70%+
- **Module complete:** all 4 lessons in the module passed
- **Level complete:** all modules in the level passed + level final exam at 80%+

Progress syncs across devices within 30 seconds of completing a lesson.

## Streak rules

| Event | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| Complete 1+ lessons in a day | Streak +1 |
| Miss 1 day | Streak unchanged (1 grace day per week) |
| Miss 2 consecutive days | Streak resets to 0 |
| Miss 3+ consecutive days | Streak resets to 0 |

Grace days reset every Monday 00:00 UTC.

## Quiz score retention

- Quiz scores are stored permanently while your account is active
- Failed quizzes (below 70%) can be retaken after a **4-hour cooldown**
- Best score per quiz is kept on your record

## Exporting progress

Go to **Settings → Progress → Download report** to export a CSV with:

- Module names and completion dates
- Quiz scores
- Total study time

CSV exports are free and unlimited.

## After cancellation

Progress is retained for **12 months** after subscription ends. Reactivate any plan within that window to restore your dashboard.
