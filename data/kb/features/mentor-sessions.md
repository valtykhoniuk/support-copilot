# Mentor Sessions

FoxSchool mentors are certified language instructors available for live 1-on-1 video sessions. Mentor sessions are an add-on available on any subscription plan.

## Pricing

| Option | Price | Details |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Single session | $20 | 45 minutes |
| 5-session pack | $90 | Saves $10; valid 90 days |

Sessions are charged at booking. Payment uses the card on file in your FoxSchool account.

## What mentors cover

- Speaking and pronunciation practice
- Homework and exercise review
- Exam preparation (DELE, DELF, Goethe, IELTS-style practice)
- Clarification of grammar from video lessons

Mentors only cover content within your **unlocked plan levels**. An Intermediate subscriber (B1–B2) cannot book C1 conversation practice.

## How to book

1. Go to **Dashboard → Book a mentor**
2. Select date and time (slots shown in your local timezone)
3. Choose a focus area from the dropdown
4. Confirm payment ($20 per session)
5. Receive calendar invite and video link by email

## Session rules

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| Duration | 45 minutes (hard stop) |
| Cancellation | Full refund if cancelled **24+ hours** before start |
| Late cancellation | No refund if cancelled less than 24 hours before |
| No-show (student) | Session forfeited, no refund |
| No-show (mentor) | Full refund + free reschedule |
| Rescheduling | Free if requested 24+ hours in advance |

## Availability

Mentor slots: Monday–Friday, 10:00–20:00 UTC. Weekend sessions are not available as of March 2026.

Average wait time for next available slot: **2 business days**.

## Refunds for mentor sessions

See [Refund Policy](../billing/refund-policy.md#mentor-session-refunds) for full details. Completed sessions are never refundable.
