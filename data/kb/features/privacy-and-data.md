# Privacy and Data

FoxSchool collects and processes personal data to provide language learning services. This article summarizes key privacy practices.

## Data we collect

| Data type | Purpose | Retention |
|-----------|---------|-----------|
| Email, name | Account and billing | Until account deletion + 30 days |
| Payment info | Processed by Stripe; FoxSchool stores last 4 digits only | Duration of subscription |
| Lesson progress, quiz scores | Learning dashboard | 12 months after cancellation |
| Video watch events | Progress sync | 12 months after cancellation |
| Support emails | Customer service | 3 years |
| Mentor session recordings | Quality assurance (with consent) | 90 days |

## Data we do not sell

FoxSchool does not sell personal data to third parties or use learning data for advertising.

## Third-party processors

| Provider | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Stripe | Payment processing |
| AWS (eu-west-1) | Hosting and video CDN |
| SendGrid | Transactional email |

## Your rights (GDPR)

EU/UK residents can:

- **Access** a copy of their data: email privacy@foxschool.app
- **Delete** their account: see [Cancel Subscription](../billing/cancel-subscription.md#account-deletion)
- **Export** progress CSV from Settings → Progress → Download report
- **Correct** email or name in Settings → Profile

Data export requests are fulfilled within **30 days**.

## Cookies

FoxSchool uses essential cookies for login sessions and optional analytics cookies. Manage preferences at [app.foxschool.app/cookies](https://app.foxschool.app/cookies).

## Children

FoxSchool requires users to be at least **16 years old**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.
