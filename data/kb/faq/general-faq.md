# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common FoxSchool questions.

## What is FoxSchool?

FoxSchool is a web-based platform for learning Spanish, French, German, or English through structured video courses aligned with CEFR levels A1–C2. You pay a monthly subscription based on the level range you need.

## How much does FoxSchool cost?

| Plan | Price | Levels |
|------|-------|--------|
| Beginner | $20/month | A1, A2 |
| Intermediate | $30/month | B1, B2 |
| Advanced | $40/month | C1, C2 |

Mentor sessions cost an additional **$20 per 45-minute session**.

## Can I get a refund?

Yes—a **full refund** is available within **7 days** of your first subscription payment. Renewal charges and completed mentor sessions are non-refundable. See [Refund Policy](../billing/refund-policy.md).

## What happens if I cancel?

Your access continues until the end of your current 30-day billing period. Progress is saved for 12 months. See [Cancel Subscription](../billing/cancel-subscription.md).

## Can I access all levels on one plan?

No. Each plan unlocks only its level range. Beginner ($20) does not include B1 content. Upgrade anytime; downgrades take effect at the next renewal.

## How do mentor sessions work?

Book a 45-minute 1-on-1 video call for $20 from your dashboard. Cancel 24+ hours ahead for a full refund. Mentors only cover content within your plan's levels.

## Is there a free trial?

FoxSchool does not offer a free trial. The placement test is free and helps you choose the right starting module and plan.

## Can I study on my phone?

Yes—FoxSchool works in mobile browsers (Chrome, Safari). There is no native app and no offline mode as of March 2026.

## How many devices can I use?

Up to **3 simultaneous sessions**. A fourth login logs out the oldest session.

## What languages can I learn?

Spanish, French, German, and English. You pick one primary language at signup. Switching costs $5 and resets progress for the new language.

## How long does it take to complete a level?

Approximate study time: A1 (60–80 hours), A2 (80–100 hours), B1 (100–120 hours), B2 (120–150 hours), C1 (150–180 hours), C2 (180–200 hours). Pace depends on you.

## Are certificates accredited?

No. Optional completion certificates ($15 per level) are PDF downloads for personal use, not accredited diplomas.

## How do I contact support?

Email support@foxschool.app, Monday–Friday 9:00–18:00 UTC. Beginner plan: 48-hour response. Intermediate: 24 hours. Advanced: 12 hours.

## My video won't play—what do I do?

See [Video Playback Issues](../troubleshooting/video-playback-issues.md). Most issues are fixed by clearing cache, disabling VPN, or verifying your email.

## I was charged twice—help!

Email support@foxschool.app with your bank statement. One charge may be a pending authorization that drops in 3–5 days. See [Payment Issues](../troubleshooting/payment-issues.md).
