# Your First Lesson on FoxSchool

After signup, your dashboard shows the first module for your plan level. Beginner subscribers start at **A1 Module 1: Greetings and Basics**.

## Starting a lesson

1. Log in at [app.foxschool.app](https://app.foxschool.app)
2. Click **Continue learning** on the dashboard hero card
3. Watch the intro video (average length: 8 minutes)
4. Complete 5 interactive exercises (drag-and-drop, multiple choice, speaking prompt)
5. Pass the module quiz with at least **70%** to unlock the next lesson

## Lesson structure

Each lesson contains:

| Component | Duration | Required |
|-----------|----------|----------|
| Video lecture | 6–12 min | Yes |
| Vocabulary flashcards | 5 min | Yes |
| Grammar exercise set | 10 min | Yes |
| Speaking practice (mic) | 3 min | Optional |
| Module quiz | 5 min | Yes (70% to pass) |

## Progress rules

- Lessons unlock **sequentially** within a module—you cannot skip ahead
- Completing A1 Module 12 unlocks A2 Module 1 (Beginner plan)
- Your streak counter increases when you finish at least one lesson per calendar day
- Streaks reset after **3 consecutive days** without activity

## Keyboard shortcuts (web)

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Space` | Play/pause video |
| `N` | Next exercise |
| `R` | Replay last 10 seconds |
| `Esc` | Exit lesson (progress saved) |

## Mentor sessions (optional)

Want live feedback? Book a 45-minute mentor session for **+$20** from **Dashboard → Book a mentor**. Sessions are available Monday–Friday, 10:00–20:00 UTC.
