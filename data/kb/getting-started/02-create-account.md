# Create Your FoxSchool Account

Signing up for FoxSchool takes about 3 minutes. You need a valid email address and a payment method to activate a paid plan.

## Step-by-step registration

1. Go to [app.foxschool.app/signup](https://app.foxschool.app/signup)
2. Enter your email and create a password (minimum 8 characters, at least one number)
3. Choose your **primary language** to study (Spanish, French, German, or English)
4. Select a subscription plan: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
5. Enter payment details (Visa, Mastercard, or PayPal)
6. Confirm your email via the link sent within 15 minutes

Your plan activates immediately after successful payment. Course access appears on your dashboard within 60 seconds.

## Account requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Minimum age | 16 years |
| Email | One account per email address |
| Devices | Up to 3 simultaneous browser sessions |
| Regions | Available in 42 countries (see full list at help.foxschool.app/regions) |

## Email verification

If you do not verify your email within 48 hours, lesson playback is paused until verification is complete. Click **Resend verification** on the login page or contact support@foxschool.app.

## Choosing the right plan

| Your situation | Recommended plan |
|----------------|------------------|
| Never studied the language | Beginner (A1–A2) |
| Completed A2 or equivalent | Intermediate (B1–B2) |
| Completed B2 or equivalent | Advanced (C1–C2) |

Unsure of your level? Take the free 15-minute placement test at [app.foxschool.app/placement](https://app.foxschool.app/placement) before subscribing.
