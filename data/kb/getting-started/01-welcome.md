# Welcome to FoxSchool

FoxSchool is a web-based language learning platform where you purchase subscription access to structured courses aligned with the CEFR levels (A1 through C2). Each plan unlocks a specific range of levels and materials; you study at your own pace through video lessons, interactive exercises, and optional live mentor sessions.

## Who FoxSchool is for

FoxSchool is built for adults who want to learn a foreign language with clear progression—from absolute beginner (A1) to near-native proficiency (C2). Whether you are preparing for travel, career growth, or personal enrichment, you choose a plan that matches your current level and upgrade when you are ready.

## Languages available

| Language | Levels offered | New content cadence |
|----------|----------------|---------------------|
| Spanish  | A1–C2          | 2 modules per month |
| French   | A1–C2          | 2 modules per month |
| German   | A1–C2          | 2 modules per month |
| English  | A1–C2          | 2 modules per month |

You select one primary language when you sign up. Switching languages costs $5 and resets your progress tracker for the new language.

## How access works

- **Beginner plan ($20/month):** A1 and A2 courses only
- **Intermediate plan ($30/month):** B1 and B2 courses only
- **Advanced plan ($40/month):** C1 and C2 courses only
- **Mentor sessions:** +$20 per 45-minute live session with a certified mentor (any plan)

All plans include unlimited replays of unlocked lessons, downloadable PDF worksheets, and a personal progress dashboard.
