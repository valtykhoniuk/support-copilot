# Refund Policy

FoxSchool offers a straightforward refund policy for first-time subscription payments. Mentor session purchases follow separate rules.

## Full refund window (subscriptions)

| Condition | Refund amount | Deadline |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| First subscription payment only | 100% | Within **7 days** of initial charge |
| Renewal payments | No refund | — |
| Upgrade proration charges | No refund | — |

The 7-day window starts at the exact timestamp of your first successful payment (UTC). Example: if you subscribed on June 1 at 14:30 UTC, you may request a full refund until June 8 at 14:30 UTC.

## How to request a refund

1. Email support@foxschool.app from your registered account email
2. Subject line: `Refund Request — [your email]`
3. Include your account email and payment date
4. Refunds process within **5 business days** to your original payment method

## Refund examples

| Scenario | Days since first payment | Refund? |
|----------|--------------------------|---------|
| Cancel on day 3, completed 2 lessons | 3 | Yes — 100% |
| Cancel on day 7, completed 10 lessons | 7 | Yes — 100% (before 7-day deadline expires) |
| Cancel on day 10 | 10 | No — outside 7-day window |
| Second monthly renewal | 35 | No — renewals are non-refundable |
| Upgraded Beginner → Intermediate on day 5 | 5 | Full refund of **original** Beginner payment only if entire account is cancelled; upgrade charge is non-refundable |

## Mentor session refunds

| Situation | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Cancelled more than 24 hours before session | 100% ($20) |
| Cancelled less than 24 hours before session | 0% |
| Mentor no-show | 100% + free reschedule |
| Session completed | 0% |

## Non-refundable items

- Certificate of completion purchases ($15)
- Language switch fee ($5)
- Mentor session packs after any session in the pack is used
- Subscription renewals (month 2 and beyond)

## Chargebacks

If you file a chargeback without contacting support first, your account is suspended pending investigation. Contact support@foxschool.app before disputing with your bank.
