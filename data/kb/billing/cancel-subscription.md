# Cancel Your Subscription

You can cancel your FoxSchool subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future renewals; you retain access until the end of your current billing period.

## How to cancel

1. Log in at [app.foxschool.app](https://app.foxschool.app)
2. Go to **Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription**
3. Select a reason (optional) and confirm
4. You receive a confirmation email within 5 minutes

Alternatively, email support@foxschool.app with subject `Cancel Subscription` from your registered email.

## What happens after cancellation

| Item | Behavior |
|------|----------|
| Course access | Active until end of current 30-day period |
| Progress data | Saved for **12 months** after cancellation |
| Mentor sessions booked | Remain valid if scheduled before period ends |
| Downloaded PDFs | Yours to keep |
| Reactivation | Log in and choose a new plan—progress restores if within 12 months |

## Cancellation vs refund

- **Cancel** = stop future charges; no automatic money back
- **Refund** = money returned; only available within [7 days of first payment](refund-policy.md)

If you are within the 7-day refund window, request a refund instead of (or in addition to) cancelling.

## Downgrade instead of cancel

If your level is too advanced or too basic, consider changing plans at **Settings → Billing → Change plan** instead of cancelling. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal date.

## Account deletion

To permanently delete your account and all data:

1. Cancel subscription first
2. Email privacy@foxschool.app with subject `Delete My Account`
3. Deletion completes within **30 days** (GDPR request)

Account deletion is irreversible and removes all progress, even within the 12-month retention window.
