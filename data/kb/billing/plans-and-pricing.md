# Plans and Pricing

FoxSchool offers three monthly subscription tiers based on CEFR level ranges. All prices are in USD and billed monthly on the same calendar day you subscribed.

## Subscription plans

| Plan | Price | Levels included | Video lessons | Worksheets | Placement test |
|------|-------|-----------------|---------------|------------|----------------|
| **Beginner** | $20/month | A1, A2 | 48 lessons | 48 PDFs | Included |
| **Intermediate** | $30/month | B1, B2 | 52 lessons | 52 PDFs | Included |
| **Advanced** | $40/month | C1, C2 | 44 lessons | 44 PDFs | Included |

### What each plan includes

- Unlimited replays of lessons within your level range
- Progress dashboard with quiz scores and streak tracking
- Email support (response within 48 hours for Beginner, 24 hours for Intermediate, 12 hours for Advanced)
- 3 simultaneous device sessions

### What is not included

- Levels outside your plan range (e.g., B1 content on the Beginner plan)
- Live mentor sessions (sold separately)
- Certificate of completion (available for $15 per level)

## Mentor sessions (add-on)

| Item | Price | Details |
|------|-------|---------|
| Single mentor session | $20 | 45 minutes, 1-on-1 video call |
| Session pack (5) | $90 | Save $10; expires in 90 days |

Mentor sessions can be booked on any plan. Mentors cover speaking practice, homework review, and exam preparation within your unlocked levels.

## Billing cycle

- First charge occurs at signup
- Renewal charges automatically every 30 days at 00:00 UTC on your billing date
- Failed payments retry after 3 days and 7 days; access suspends on day 8 until payment succeeds

## Upgrading or downgrading

| Change | Effect | Price adjustment |
|--------|--------|------------------|
| Upgrade (e.g., Beginner → Intermediate) | Immediate access to new levels | Prorated charge for remaining days |
| Downgrade | Takes effect at next renewal | No mid-cycle refund for level difference |
| Cancel | See [Cancel Subscription](cancel-subscription.md) | — |

## Annual billing

FoxSchool does not offer annual plans as of March 2026. All subscriptions are month-to-month.
